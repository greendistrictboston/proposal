A Proposal by Jack Botein
The Green District, Boston MA
The Concept
The Green District is a proposal for a carbon nuetral, 100% affordable new city district home to 10,000 Bostonians. Situated on 22 acres of land reclaimed from the Bass River (an inlet of Boston Harbor), the district will be comprised of 33 12 story buildings, with independent retailers anchoring each ground floor, and 11 stories of 100% income restricted rental units. The Green District has zero net carbon dioxide emmissions, with all power generated from photovoltaic solar panels on all 33 building's roofs, and a central geothermal well providing heating and cooling. This new urban district will significantly reduce Boston's affordable housing crunch, while also achieving full carbon neutrality.
